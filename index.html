Who We Are

Basically, fans of Javascript. From newbies to working developers, we've all got one thing in common: We think Javascript is pretty awesome, and we want to learn and write more, better code using the tools JS provides.

What We Do

On Wednesdays, we get together and write Javascript. We cover all kinds of topics, but aim to approach them in ways that are accessible and beneficial to anyone.

How to Help

Come learn with us. Lend us your questions, suggestions, projects and ideas.
