Who We Are

The JavaScript Guild is a group of General Assembly Alumni who meet every Wednesday to explore all things JavaScript. Ben Haaland and Victor Salcedo founded the group in the fall of 2014. It has since expanded into two sections and over thirty members.

Guild Member? Add your idea to the agenda for a session!

What We Do

The JS Guild works through the Eloquent JavaScript book by Marijn Haverbeke. We also do outside projects as a group and with others in the tech community.

How to Help

We love having JS masters come and teach us new things! We also love getting noob's started! For more information on the Guild, please contact us or visit our GitHub page!
